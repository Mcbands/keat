Brief History

Keres Engineering and Appliance Technology (KEAT) was established in 2022 with a primary focus on providing specialized sewing machine repair services. Originating from a background in industrial maintenance supervision at a leading company, KEAT quickly gained recognition for its commitment to quality and excellence in repair services.

Over time, driven by a growing demand for broader engineering solutions, KEAT expanded its offerings to include comprehensive electrical engineering services and diversified appliance repairs. Today, KEAT stands as a trusted provider of appliance repair services, specializing in a wide range of household appliances.

In addition to our repair expertise, KEAT is dedicated to enhancing professional development within the engineering community through our Continuous Professional Development (CPD) courses. These courses are designed to equip individuals with the skills necessary to excel in various engineering fields, ensuring they remain at the forefront of industry advancements.

From humble beginnings in the sewing machine repair industry to a multifaceted engineering and appliance technology company, KEAT continues to uphold its core values of excellence, innovation, and community impact. Our journey reflects our unwavering dedication to delivering reliable, high-quality services and empowering individuals through education and training.
